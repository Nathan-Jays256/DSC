Who We Are.

DCS is a premier management and strategy consulting firm that recognizes, supports, facilitates and promotes research, accounting, auditing and innovations as an engine of development in Africa.

DCS aims to be the consultancy provider of choice for institutions/organisations seeking prudent financial, accounting and auditing standards, the generation of knowledge and innovations, dissemination, uptake and commercialization of research outputs and innovations within Africa.

We exists offer professional consultancy service with expertise in management, policy and planning, budgeting, accounting, auditing and financial analysis. We also undertake research, grant proposal writing and grant management, performance reporting coupled with a deep understanding of the research policy and funding environment.
